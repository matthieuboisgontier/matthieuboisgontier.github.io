I am passionate about the neuroscience of physical inactivity and sensorimotor control. My education includes master’s degrees in Physical Therapy, Kinesiology, and Engineering at the Université Grenoble Alpes (France). I finished a Ph.D. in Neuroscience at the National Center for Scientific Research (CNRS) in France with a focus on aging, which was also one of the topics of my postdoctorate at KU Leuven (Belgium). During this postdoc, I was fortunate to learn neuroimaging techniques such as Magnetic Resonance Imaging and advanced statistics such as mixed-effects models and to establish multiple international collaborations in Canada, Europe, and USA.
