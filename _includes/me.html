Matt is a neuroscientist, kinesiologist, and physiotherapist working in the field of physical inactivity, movement control, and aging. He achieved a PhD from the University Grenoble Alpes (France) and did a 6-year postdoc at KU Leuven (Belgium) including a 1-year visit at the University of British Columbia (Canada), both supported by the Research Foundation - Flanders (FWO). During this postdoc, he has also been collaborating with the University of Geneva (Switzerland).
