Matt is a neuroscientist, a kinesiologist, and a physiotherapist. He achieved a PhD from the University of Grenoble in 2012 (France). Then, he did a 6-year postdoc at Leuven University (Belgium) including a 1-year visit at the University of British Columbia (Canada), both supported by the Research Foundation - Flanders (FWO). Since 2015, he has been collaborating with the University of Geneva (Switzerland).
